What is to be Corinthians?

Several teams stars, such as Ronaldo and Socrates, while giving a few of interviews, say that nobody can explain what is to be Corinthians, they tell is a unique feeling. Maybe there are some events that makes clear what they're trying to explain, for example, the day that thousands of fans went to Sao Paulo's international airport to give support for the team members that were flying to Japan for the world championship.
